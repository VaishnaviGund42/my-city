Famous Temples in Pandharpur

Vitthal-Rukmini Temple

This is the main temple of Pandharpur dedicated to Lord Vitthal and Rukmini Devi. It is the heart of Warkari devotion and visited by millions during Ashadhi and Kartiki Ekadashi.

Pundalik Temple

ISKCON Temple

A modern and serene temple built by the International Society for Krishna Consciousness. It offers peaceful ambiance, discourses, and prasadam.

Shri Gopalpur Temple

This is where Lord Krishna stayed during His visit to Pandharpur. It is situated a few kilometers from the main city.

Chandrabhaga River Ghat

Devotees take a holy dip here before visiting Vitthal Temple. The crescent-shaped river is sacred and deeply spiritual for pilgrims.
