About Pandharpur

Pandharpur is a pilgrimage city on the banks of the Chandrabhaga River in Maharashtra. It is famous for the Vithoba temple, attracting lakhs of devotees during Ashadhi Ekadashi and Kartiki Ekadashi.

Famous Places

  • Shree Vitthal Rukmini Mandir
  • Chandrabhaga River
  • Pundalik Temple
  • ISKCON Temple
